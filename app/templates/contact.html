Common questions

What kind of projects do you take on?

I work on web apps, SaaS platforms, e-commerce sites, and internal tools. I'm most useful when both design quality and engineering depth matter — not just one or the other.

How long does a typical project take?

A landing page or small app: 2–4 weeks. A full-featured SaaS or e-commerce product: 8–16 weeks. I'll give you a detailed timeline before any work begins.

Do you do design as well as development?

Yes — I work in Figma and can handle the full design-to-code pipeline. For larger branding projects I partner with dedicated designers I trust.

What's your preferred tech stack?

React / Next.js on the frontend, Node.js or Python on the backend, PostgreSQL for data, and Vercel or AWS for deployment. Happy to adapt if your team already has a stack.
